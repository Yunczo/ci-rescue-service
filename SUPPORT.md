## Boundary

Tips are optional and never required to use any free resource. They are not a purchase, preorder, charitable contribution, or payment for CI Rescue service. They buy no service, diagnosis, response, delivery, support, priority, refund, or other consideration. Do not include secrets or personal data in a public transaction note.

To request the fixed-price repair service, use the [service-request intake](https://github.com/Yunczo/ci-rescue-service/issues/new?template=service-request.yml) and do not pay until the request is accepted in writing with an exact BTC quote.
